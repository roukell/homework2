Welcome to my portfolio.
I am a Web Developer based in Sydney, Australia, experience in HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, API, Node.js, React.js, mySQL, Bootstrap, and responsive design.
My passion for web development can be traced back to my pre-professinol days.
I remember my very first time building a website was in middle school, we had an instructor teaching us how to build a blog.
The excitment of being able to build and be creative, was something that I would never forget.
Although I had somehow lost track of web development, I realised that it is important to work with something that is enjoyable.
That's why after many years of working in medical imaging, I have decided to get back to web development.
My background in medical has helped me developed strong problem solving skills, ability to work with strict deadlines, and outstanding time managment skills.
I am highly motivated to create functional websites, and looking forward to build more incredible projects.
